What you'll do

we would like to show you the amazing underwater world around the Red Sea , Aqaba. Your tour will start as we pick you up from our meeting point , from there we will head to the diving point around 20 min by car. whether you are first-time diver or a pro, we will introduce you to the diving in the area. when we arrive at the dive site a briefing will be made to you explaining the equipment and how to use it, basic scuba diving skills,hands signals,also the safety aspects of Scuba diving.
dive will be from the beach and will last approximately 45 minutes.
